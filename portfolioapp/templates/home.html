About Me

I am a passionate professional teacher of Mathematics and Computing, trained at NJA College of Education. My journey into education was driven by a deep desire to empower young minds through logical reasoning, problem-solving, and digital literacy. Throughout my training and professional practice, I discovered that education is not only about imparting knowledge but also about inspiring innovation and transformation.

While teaching provided me with a strong foundation in communication, leadership, and mentorship, I soon realized that my true passion lay at the intersection of education and technology. I became fascinated by the idea that, beyond using digital tools in the classroom, I could actually build these tools to enhance learning experiences and solve real-world challenges. This realization marked the beginning of my transition into software development.

As a self-taught AI-assisted Full-Stack Developer, I have dedicated countless hours to mastering modern web technologies. I specialize in building scalable and user-centered applications using Django, React, and Tailwind CSS. My development approach combines analytical thinking from my mathematics background with creativity and empathy gained from my teaching experience, enabling me to design solutions that are both functional and impactful.

During my time at college, I also served as the SRC Public Relations Officer, a role that strengthened my leadership, teamwork, and communication skills. This experience taught me that the most successful technological solutions are not merely defined by their code but by their ability to address real human needs and foster meaningful connections within communities.

My journey into technology has been significantly enhanced through the use of Artificial Intelligence tools, which have accelerated my learning and enabled me to tackle complex development challenges with confidence. As a certified AI-assisted developer and an ethical hacking mentee, I am committed to building secure, intelligent, and scalable digital systems that contribute positively to society.

I am particularly passionate about developing “Technology with a Purpose”—solutions that empower students, support educators, and modernize everyday life. Whether it is creating educational platforms, community-driven applications, or innovative digital tools, my goal is to bridge the gap between pedagogy and technology.

Beyond my professional pursuits, I am a lifelong learner with a keen interest in personal and intellectual growth. I have been exploring new languages, including Chinese, to broaden my global perspective and enhance cross-cultural communication. This commitment to continuous learning reflects my belief that growth and adaptability are essential in today’s rapidly evolving digital world.

As I graduate in September, I look forward to contributing my unique blend of educational expertise and technological innovation to organizations that value creativity, impact, and community development. I am eager to collaborate on projects that leverage technology to inspire change, improve access to education, and create meaningful digital experiences.

In essence, my journey represents a seamless fusion of education, technology, and community impact. I am not just a teacher or a developer—I am an innovator committed to shaping a smarter and more inclusive future through purposeful technology.

Every project I undertake reflects my dedication to excellence, usability, and social impact. I believe that technology should be accessible, inclusive, and transformative. By combining my teaching background with modern software engineering practices, I strive to create digital solutions that make a lasting difference in people’s lives.

If you are looking for a professional who blends pedagogical insight with technical expertise to build intelligent and meaningful solutions, I would be delighted to connect and collaborate.
